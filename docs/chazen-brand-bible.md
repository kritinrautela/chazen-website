# CHAZEN Brand Bible and Homepage Plan

## Brand Bible

CHAZEN 茶禪 is a luxury digital tea house: part cultural museum, part ritual guide, part meditation room, part meaningful gifting brand. The brand speaks with restraint. It never sells tea as commodity; it presents tea as a return to attention, culture, and breath.

Primary line:
One Cup. One Breath. One Return.

Chinese line:
一盞茶，
一口氣，
回到當下。

Tone:
Quiet, precise, scholarly, tactile, and warm. Avoid wellness cliches, generic luxury claims, cartoon motifs, fake gold effects, and fast-commerce language.

Visual language:
Song dynasty restraint, Japanese spatial discipline, museum captions, Aman-like calm, Aesop-like editorial density, Apple-level spacing, aged bronze, ink, moss, clay, porcelain, paper, and warm shadow.

## Design System

Colors:
- Porcelain: #fbfaf6
- Warm ivory: #f2eadc
- Ink black: #171713
- Tea brown: #6f4a32
- Aged bronze: #9d8150
- Moss green: #3f5945
- Clay seal red: #9f3f2f
- Deep charcoal: #10120f

Typography:
- Display: Georgia / Cormorant-style serif for cultural calm and bilingual hierarchy.
- Interface: Inter / Avenir-style sans for navigation, captions, and museum labels.
- Letter spacing remains natural; use small caps only for labels.

Layout:
- Large negative space.
- Full-bleed first viewport.
- Text placed directly on space or image, not inside marketing cards.
- Cards only for discrete objects or collection entries.
- Border radius at 8px or below.

## Homepage Wireframe

1. Full-bleed arrival hero: brand, tagline, Chinese line, photographic tea-room atmosphere, ceramic/steam/singing bowl details.
2. Philosophy prologue: tea as ritual, culture, meditation, return.
3. Tea table preview: top-down ritual objects with labels.
4. Ritual scroll preview: six brewing moments as a quiet timeline.
5. Twelve-chapter index: shows the future structure without building every chapter.
6. Gifting house: settlement, corporate, VIP, family blessing.
7. Closing mission: CHAZEN as a modern cultural tea house.

## Cultural Content Plan

Use bilingual names only where they add meaning. Treat Chinese tea tools, Song aesthetics, and archetypes as cultural material, not decoration. Each future chapter should pair object-level specificity with a short ritual or reflective use case.

## Motion Direction

Motion must feel like breath, steam, and slow shadow:
- Hero content fades and rises gently.
- Steam drifts upward with low opacity.
- Tea table objects respond subtly on hover.
- Section reveals use short, restrained Framer Motion transitions.
- No bouncy easing, parallax spectacle, or fast carousel behavior.
